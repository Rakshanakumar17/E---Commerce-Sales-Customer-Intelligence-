📊 E-Commerce Sales & Customer Intelligence Dashboard

An end-to-end data analytics project analyzing the Global Superstore
dataset using Python, Excel, and Tableau to uncover sales, profit, and
customer insights, presented through an interactive Tableau dashboard.

🔍 Overview

This project walks through a complete analytics workflow — from raw data to
a business-ready dashboard — exploring sales performance across categories,
regions, and customer segments to support data-driven decision-making.

🔹 Project Workflow


✅ Imported and explored the dataset using Python (Google Colab)
✅ Cleaned and prepared the data using Pandas and Excel
✅ Performed Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
✅ Built visualizations for monthly trends and top customers
✅ Designed an interactive Tableau dashboard with key business KPIs

📊 Key Insights


Technology leads both sales and profit — ₹4.74M in sales and 663,779
in profit, ahead of Furniture and Office Supplies.
Furniture sells well but underperforms on profit — despite generating
₹4.11M in sales (2nd highest), it produced the lowest profit (285,205)
of the three categories, suggesting thin margins worth investigating.
Consumer segment drives the business — Consumers account for 51.48%
of sales, more than Corporate (30.25%) and Home Office (18.27%) combined,
making it the single most important segment to serve well.
Central region is the top performer — leading all regions with
2,822,303 in sales, well ahead of North Asia (848,310) and Africa (783,773).
Sales grew steadily through the year — the monthly trend shows a dip
mid-year before recovering and climbing to its highest point by year-end.
Customer concentration — the top 10 customers (led by Tom Ashbrook and
Tamara Chand) each contribute significant repeat revenue, highlighting the
value of key account retention.
